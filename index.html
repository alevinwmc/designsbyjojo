About Jojo

One pair of hands

I'm into something good. That's how I feel when I'm creating. I love molding soft, squishy clay into playful shapes that more often than not become hearts and flowers. The process is meditative, like a quiet conversation with the clay that slows life down. I began throwing three years ago, and what started as curiosity quickly became an obsession. Now at 70, I continue to explore forms and surface design with a joyful, experimental approach, showing and selling work locally and through Instagram, deepening my practice, and my love for it, one piece at a time. Joanne Levin
